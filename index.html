C.G.C landran

CGC Landran, with its 23-year legacy under the aegis of Sri Guru Ramdass Educational Society, has established itself as one of the leading higher education institutes. With NAAC A+ accreditation, NIRF, NBA, ARIIA, IIC, and other prestigious rankings it is one of the most sought-after institutes in the Northern region. CGC Landran is committed to equipping students with an affordable quality education that includes theoretical knowledge and hands-on learning equipping them with rich academics and experiential learning opportunities. CGC Landran has been at the forefront of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship, especially with initiatives such as the Atal Community Innovation Centre (ACIC) RISE Association, supported by AIM, and NITI Aayog (Govt. of India), which seeks to inculcate the spirit of entrepreneurship in students, supporting and encouraging them to ideate and setup their start-ups. Being among the heritage institutes in the region CGC Landran is committed to providing the best of faculty, infrastructure, global associations, and a safe and culturally diverse campus, making the students participate in sports, skill upgrading activities, clubs, committees, NCC, NSS, etc beyond academics. Robust Industrial associations with the best names in the industry, 360-degree training, international study opportunities, and employability-enhancing programs at CGC Landran ensure the students grab the best placement opportunities across streams at astounding packages.
